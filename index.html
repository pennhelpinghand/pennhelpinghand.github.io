HelpingHand More Details

Strengthening support networks for people with substance abuse disorders.

HelpingHand was a healthtech platform we created for people with substance abuse disorders to easily receive support from their loved ones. We created a FitBit app with a heart rate monitor to detect signs of substance abuse withdrawals or relapse. The accompanying app keeps track of healthy habits.

Loved ones receive a SMS notification when biosignals signal a potential withdrawal or relapse while reminding them to celebrate recovery milestones.

Together, Helpinghand complements the substance abuser's existing support network, strengthening it. Allowing them a stronger source of accountability and positive reinforcement.

This was our project for PennApps XX @ the University of Pennsylvania where HelpingHand was ranked top 30 out of almost 300 teams.
